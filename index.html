Welcome to Roommates

Welcome to our sunny room for rent in Valencia, Spain! We are excited to offer you a comfortable and functional living space that combines convenience, compromise, and location.

As your hosts, we take great pride in ensuring that our living space is clean, well-maintained, and equipped with all the necessary amenities to make your stay enjoyable. Here are some important things you should know:

  • House Rules: Basic house rules to ensure comfortable and peaceful living.
  • Rent and Bill Due Dates: Clear due dates to help manage finances and avoid late fees.
  • Cleaning Schedule: A schedule to ensure common areas are kept clean and tidy.

We offer a range of amenities including high-speed internet, secure locks, smoke and carbon monoxide detectors, comfortable furniture, and more. Our living space is located in the heart of Valencia, just minutes away from the beach and top universities.

Contact us today to schedule a tour and live your dream life in sunny Valencia, Spain!
